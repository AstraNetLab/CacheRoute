Hot Pot, also known as "guo zi", "huan lu" or "da bian lu". Anciently known as "Antique Soup", it is a characteristic delicacy that combines traditional Chinese dining utensils with cooking methods. Ingredients are boiled in boiling water or soup base and eaten immediately after cooking. It includes copper pot hot pot with meat, spicy hot pot, and yin-yang hot pot, etc. Common ingredients cover meat, seafood, vegetables and bean products.

Hot pot is classified by quality into copper, aluminum, pottery, enamel, etc. According to the fuel used, they can be classified into charcoal hot pot, alcohol hot pot, gas hot pot and electric hot pot, etc.

The convenient and fast popular characteristics of hot pot catering have been accepted and loved by consumers. Especially in the cold and dry weather of northern regions, the potential of the hot pot market is huge, and the business models of hot pot types are also constantly innovating.
